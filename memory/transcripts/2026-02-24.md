### Ghost GDP — This Is the Key Concept

Output that shows up in national accounts but **never circulates through the real economy.** Companies show productivity gains (nominal GDP looks fine), but machines don't spend money. GDP becomes a mirage — the number goes up while the actual economy hollows out underneath.

- **Labor's share of GDP:** 64% (1974) → 56% (2024) → **46% (2028)**
